Janurary 24th, 2019: Book Reflections and Comments:

Now that I finished "A Course In Miracles", (as if it could ever be finished), I'm currently reading three new books:

1. "The Convoluted Universe" by Dolores Canon,

2. "The Book of Freedom" by Paul Selig, and

3. "Love And A Map to the Unaltered Soul" by Tina Spalding.

So far, the beginning of Dolore's book has the typical hypnosis sessions and talk about past lives, but the table of contents does show diverse topics to come towards the back of the book (that I have not read yet).

The next book, "The Book of Freedom" by Paul Selig starts out a little slow but ramps up with unique wording and perspectives around the two-hundred page section. At this point in the book, (considering I haven't finished), it has a feeling of increasing-high-energy and an opening to new possibilities and great adventure. Without giving away too many details of the book... we will see where the last half of the book takes us...

Finally, we have Tina Spalding's book called "Love and A Map to the Unaltered Soul." When I think of her book, the word that comes to mind is "concrete". It's a great book! It's grounded and the concepts are explained in detail. It is as if every word of truth brings peace and joy over your entire body. Of course, I might be a little biased because I followed her on youtube with her Course In Miracles sessions, but as far as I can tell... even a non-course in Miracles Pratcitioner could fully enjoy the concepts her book. . . The wording is simple and the concepts are real and easy to understand.
